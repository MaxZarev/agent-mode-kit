# AI Writing Tropes

Full catalog of AI writing tells. Source: tropes.fyi + slop-forensics research.

## Word Choice

### "Quietly" and Magic Adverbs
"quietly orchestrating", "deeply transformative", "fundamentally reshaping" — adverbs to inject false significance.
**RU:** "тихо формируя", "глубоко трансформируя", "кардинально меняя"

### "Delve" and Friends
delve, certainly, utilize, leverage, robust, streamline, harness — the classic AI vocabulary.
**RU:** заглянуть (в тему), безусловно, задействовать, использовать потенциал

### "Tapestry" and "Landscape"
Ornate nouns where simple words work: tapestry, landscape, paradigm, synergy, ecosystem, framework.
**RU:** палитра, ландшафт, парадигма, синергия, экосистема, фреймворк

### The "Serves As" Dodge
"serves as", "stands as", "marks", "represents" — replacing simple "is".
**RU:** "служит", "выступает в качестве", "представляет собой" — say "это" or "является".

## Sentence Structure

### Negative Parallelism
"It's not X — it's Y." The single most common AI tell. One per piece max, if at all.
**RU:** "Это не X — это Y."

### "Not X. Not Y. Just Z."
Dramatic countdown. State Z directly.

### "The X? A Y."
Self-posed rhetorical questions. "The result? Devastating."
**RU:** "Результат? Сокрушительный."

### Superficial Analyses
Tacking "-ing" phrases: "highlighting its importance", "reflecting broader trends", "contributing to development".
**RU:** "подчёркивая его значимость", "отражая общие тенденции", "способствуя развитию"

### False Ranges
"From X to Y" where no real scale exists. "From innovation to cultural transformation."
**RU:** "От инноваций до культурной трансформации."

### "It's Worth Noting"
Filler transition. Delete.
**RU:** "Стоит отметить" — delete.

## Tone

### "Here's the Kicker"
False suspense. "Here's the thing", "Here's where it gets interesting."
**RU:** "А вот что интересно", "Но самое главное"

### "Think of It As..."
Patronizing analogy. "Think of it like a highway for data."
**RU:** "Представьте себе это как...", "Это можно сравнить с..."

### "Imagine a World Where..."
Classic AI futurism opener.
**RU:** "Представьте мир, в котором..."

### False Vulnerability
Simulated self-awareness. "And yes, I'm openly biased here."
**RU:** "Да, я тут субъективен, но..."

### Grandiose Stakes Inflation
Everything is world-historical. API pricing becomes "defining the next era of computing."
**RU:** "определит будущее целой индустрии", "изменит всё, что мы знали о..."

### "Let's Break This Down"
Pedagogical voice. "Let's unpack this", "Let's explore", "Let's dive in."
**RU:** "Давайте разберёмся", "Давайте рассмотрим подробнее"

### Vague Attributions
"Experts argue...", "Industry reports suggest..." — if you can't name them, you don't have a source.
**RU:** "Эксперты утверждают...", "По мнению специалистов..."

### Invented Concept Labels
"supervision paradox", "acceleration trap" — sounds analytical but not grounded.
**RU:** "парадокс контроля", "ловушка ускорения" — fake-analytical compound labels.

## Formatting

### Em-Dash Addiction
Humans: 2-3 per piece. AI: 20+. Remove them.

### Bold-First Bullets
**Keyword**: followed by description. Almost nobody formats this way by hand. Strong AI signal.

### Unicode Decoration
→ arrows, "smart quotes", special characters. Use straight quotes and ->.

## Composition

### Fractal Summaries
"What I'm going to tell you; what I'm telling you; what I just told you." Say it once.

### The Dead Metaphor
Same metaphor beaten 5-10 times through the piece. Use once, move on.

### Historical Analogy Stacking
"Apple didn't build Uber. Facebook didn't build Spotify..." — listing companies for false authority.
**RU:** "Apple не создавал Uber. Facebook не создавал Spotify..."

### One-Point Dilution
One argument restated in 10 ways across thousands of words. Cut the repetitions.

### "Despite Its Challenges..."
Acknowledge problems → immediately dismiss. Either take challenges seriously or don't mention them.
**RU:** "Несмотря на все трудности..." → either discuss them or skip.
